Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What does a startup recruiting agency do?

A startup recruiting agency helps early-stage and scaling companies find and hire top talent quickly—especially for technical, executive, and hard-to-fill roles.

How fast can you fill a role?

We typically identify strong, qualified candidates within the first week. From there, your internal interview and onboarding process will determine the final timeline. In many cases, clients are able to have a new hire start within 2–3 weeks.

For executive or highly specialized roles, timelines may vary—but we'll always provide a realistic estimate upfront.

What is scalable HR support?

Scalable HR support means flexible, outsourced human resources expertise that grows with your business. It's fractional HR—brought in on a part-time or as-needed basis—to help startups stay compliant, onboard effectively, and build strong people systems.

Some seasons require more support. Some require less.

With scalable HR, you can adjust up or down—without the cost of a full-time hire.

What industries do you work with?

We work mostly with tech and tech-adjacent companies—especially software, AI, cybersecurity, digital health, and climate tech—but we're not industry-snobs.

We primarily work with growth-stage and mid-market organizations across technology and professional services. Our industry experience includes software and SaaS, AI and data, cybersecurity, digital health and medical technology, climate tech and clean energy, fintech, and mission-driven nonprofits.

Is Tangerine Search a San Francisco Recruiting Agency?

We are! Tangerine Search is a boutique recruiting and HR firm rooted in the San Francisco Bay Area. While many of our clients are local, we also partner with teams across the U.S. and around the world.

Can you help with international hiring or remote teams?

Definitely. We've supported distributed teams across multiple countries and time zones — hiring both full-time employees and contractors. Whether you're building a fully remote team or expanding internationally, we can help you do it thoughtfully and compliantly.

How much does it cost to work with Tangerine Search?

Our pricing depends on the type of support you need — whether that's contingent recruiting, retained executive search, HR consulting, or RPO. Most engagements start between $3,000–$5,000, with recruiting fees based on a percentage of first-year salary.
